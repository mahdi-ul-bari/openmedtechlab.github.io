Current Research Themes

1. Decentralized Learning Frameworks

Developing secure and efficient federated learning models that can operate across untrusted nodes while maintaining data privacy and integrity. Focus areas include homomorphic encryption integration.

2. Bio-Inspired Optimization

Applying principles from swarm intelligence and neural plasticity to create self-healing and adaptive system schedulers for cloud computing environments.

3. Quantum-Resistant Consensus

Investigating and prototyping novel blockchain consensus mechanisms that are resilient against future quantum computing attacks, leveraging lattice-based cryptography.
